# Asset Requirements for "For My Sindhu"

## Garden Scene (5 images)
- `garden_01.webp` - Initial view (warm sunset)
- `garden_02.webp` - Tap 1 progression
- `garden_03.webp` - Tap 2 progression
- `garden_04.webp` - Tap 3 progression
- `garden_05.webp` - Tap 4 progression (final before beach)

**Specs**: 1920x1080, WEBP format, 200-300KB each

## Beach Scene (5 images)
- `beach_01.webp` through `beach_05.webp`

## Café Scene (4 images)
- `cafe_01.webp` through `cafe_04.webp`

## Taj Mahal Scene (4 images)
- `taj_01.webp` through `taj_04.webp`

## Hilltop Scene (4 images)
- `hill_01.webp` through `hill_04.webp`

## Audio Files

### Music (128kbps MP3)
- `piano.mp3` - Garden scene
- `waves.mp3` - Beach scene (can double as ambience)
- `rain.mp3` - Café scene
- `strings.mp3` - Taj Mahal scene
- `wind.mp3` - Hilltop scene

### SFX (64kbps MP3)
- `breathing.mp3` - Opening sequence
- `typewriter.mp3` - Opening text
- `footstep.mp3` - Garden stepping stones
- `match.mp3` - Candle lighting
- `bottle.mp3` - Beach bottle (optional)

### Voice
- `voice-note.mp3` - Personal recording (96kbps MP3)

## Notes
- All images must be optimized WEBP format
- Audio files should be compressed to specified bitrates
- Place files in respective folders under `assets/`
